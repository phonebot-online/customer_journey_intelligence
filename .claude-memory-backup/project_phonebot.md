Phonebot — Australian phone retailer (refurbished/new). User is hands-on operator running ads + analytics + ops themselves.

Scale: $500,000+ AUD ad spend over trailing 12 months across Google Ads (incl Shopping), Bing, Meta. Email via Brevo. GMB locations, Search Console, GA4 (~25k events/day from one Web stream). Profit Metrics integrated for true GP attribution.

**Why:** User explicitly stated success metric = net profitability, not ROAS or revenue growth. Wants to know what questions they aren't asking, what big corporates do with this data, and how AI can help design bespoke advantages.

**How to apply:** Frame analysis recommendations around contribution margin / net profit, not vanity metrics. Account for the trap of last-click ROAS hiding cannibalization (especially branded search spend). Treat refurb/electronics economics seriously — thin margins, real inventory constraints, repurchase cycles measured in years not weeks. When suggesting tools/builds, prefer self-built BQ + dashboard work over buying SaaS unless the math clearly favors buy.
